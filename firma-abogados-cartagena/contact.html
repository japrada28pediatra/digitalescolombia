Cuéntanos tu caso

Share the basics, the timing, and any deadline you are facing so the first reply can be focused and practical. If there is a court date, a contract issue, or a family matter, note it clearly so the team can prioritise correctly.

The more context you provide, the easier it is to send the right person, prepare the right documents, and avoid unnecessary back and forth.

If you are unsure what to include, just share the type of issue and the time frame so the first reply can point you in the right direction.

That is often enough to start a useful conversation and decide whether the matter needs a call, a meeting, or a document review first. It also helps the team respond with the right level of detail instead of sending a generic reply. Small details often make the first exchange much easier.
